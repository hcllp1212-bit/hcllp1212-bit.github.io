We offer highly reliable tax preparation services

Tax preparation is a systematic process with specific protocols for returns, filings, and amendments — from income and business taxes to state & local, estate, and more. The rules are complex; our role is to make them clear and manageable.

We emphasize careful planning and proactive communication so clients avoid costly missteps and unnecessary scrutiny. With Aiken & Sanders, you get efficiency that respects your time and transparency that provides peace of mind.

Our tax services include

A wide range of service areas tailored to your situation.

Income Tax

Preparation and filing for individuals and businesses, with a focus on accuracy and timeliness.

International Tax

Cross-border reporting, treaty considerations, and planning for global operations or investments.

Business & Personal Tax

Entity selection, quarterly estimates, and annual compliance for corporations, partnerships, and individuals.

State & Local Tax

Sales & use, B&O, and other state or municipal requirements across jurisdictions.

Not-for-Profit Tax (990)

Form 990 series preparation and support for boards on disclosures and best practices.

Tax Accounting & Reporting

Methods, elections, and strategies that align books and returns while managing risk.

Tax Planning

Year-round planning to optimize deductions, credits, and cash-flow impacts.

Estate Planning

Coordination with advisors on estate, gift, and trust strategies to protect what matters.

The value of good tax services

Professional services can feel expensive — but the cost of errors, penalties, and missed opportunities is higher. As a practical and affordable alternative, many clients choose to outsource their tax preparation and select tax functions to us.

We provide virtual, remote, and on-site options nationwide. Our goal is straightforward: quality, results-oriented filings that fit your budget and maximize what you keep when the return is done.
